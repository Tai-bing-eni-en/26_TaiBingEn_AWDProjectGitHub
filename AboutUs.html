Learn more about CSB - The Little Buses

Company History

CSB - The Little Buses was newly established on 30th April 2026, which is also the company owner's birthday. What started as a passion project inspired by Sunshine Islands Bus Simulator in Roblox has grown into a dedicated provider of premium toy bus models.

Comprehensive Overview

CityScape Bus (CSB) is a Hong Kong online bus driving simulation game-based bus operator company, originating from Sunshine Islands Bus Simulator in Roblox. Our company specializes in selling high-quality toy bus models that capture the excitement and authenticity of bus driving simulation.

We combine our passion for bus driving simulations with detailed, collectible toy models that bring the gaming experience to life.

🎯 Our Mission
